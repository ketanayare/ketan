Master Of Computer Application

   Computers are becoming a part and parcel of all human endeavours and it is small wonder that a career in computers shows unmatched promise, potential and a veritable goldmine in terms of innovation and incredibly bright future prospects. MCA is the ‘flagship’ degree that can be considered as the most, coveted amongst the options available, if software is what one is zeroing in on. The Post Graduate Programme in Computer Application is essentially aimed at equipping a student with excellent programming and system related competencies. MCA is a three year (6 semesters) professional Master’s Degree in Computer Application. Students are given exposure to live industrial projects which increases their employability in software industry. The Department of MCA was established in 2004. The current intake is 60 seats. It has experienced faculty- student ratio of 1:15. 224 students have graduated from the Department. One of the students Ms. Yogini Nalwade secured second rank in University of Mumbai.

Admission To Enginering and MCA

Master of Computer Application Department is committed to:

Creating leaders and next generation trend-setters for the IT Industry.Offering state-of-the-art education and imparting other that will give students an edge in their profession think innovatively to develop better IT applications. Training individuals who would contribute substantially to the ambitious IT goals of the country. Contributing to developmental projects in government and public sectors, with industry partners, to demonstrate application of IT and gain rich experience that can be brought back into education and research. Providing training of software project development throughout the semesters.
